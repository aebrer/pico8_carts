Author's Notes

This is the generator for my pico_punks. I was heavily inspired by Max Capacity's dos_punks to see if I could create something similar on the Pico-8 Fantasy Console. I had a ton of fun making this, and I hope you have a ton of fun looking through the generated space.

Some notes:

  • All the features are programmatically generated... as in, there are no images. It's all procedural. Everything is made by stacking the Pico-8's characters over and over. Nothing but text is being displayed on the screen.
  • If you have synced your wallet, the generator will start you off on the custom pico_punk that is based on your wallet address seed.
  • There are some rare combinations and features, for example, a 1/1000 rainbow mode.

Controls:

  • Left/Right → -/+ 1 seed
  • Down/Up → -/+ 10 seeds
  • Circle Button → Random Seed
  • X Button → Save a downloadable image
