About

This is the original pico_punks generator. Heavily inspired by Max Capacity's dos_punks, I wanted to see if I could create something similar on the Pico-8 Fantasy Console‚Äîfully procedural avatar generation using nothing but code.

All the features are programmatically generated. There are no images, no sprites, no pre-rendered components. Everything is made by stacking Pico-8's built-in characters over and over. Nothing but text is being displayed on the screen, composed into faces through procedural generation and completed by your brain through pareidolia.

Series Evolution

This first generator explored the space of what's possible with procedural generation on heavily constrained hardware. Each punk is isolated: one seed, one process, one output.

Over time this evolved into pico_punk_generator_generator.p8, where the curator isn't just choosing from infinite punks‚Äîthe process of exploration itself modifies the output. That final version was featured in the "Digital Self" exhibition at Jano Lapin Gallery (September-November 2022).

What began as satire became identity. My profile picture across platforms is now a pico_punk‚Äînot just a representation of identity, but constitutive of it.
