Tweetcarts

Code golf as art form—generative pieces constrained to 280 characters or less. Working within extreme limitations to create emergence from minimal code.

Tweetcarts are a unique form of constraint-based generative art. The entire source code must fit into a single tweet (280 characters maximum). Within this brutal limitation, artists create everything from particle simulations to fire effects to geometric patterns. The constraint forces elegant solutions and creative problem-solving.
