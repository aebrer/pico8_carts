Vestiges

Explorations of remnants, traces, and what remains after something has passed.

The Vestiges series examines themes of memory, forgetting, and what's left behind. These pieces often feature minimalist palettes, particle systems, entropy locking, and slow evolution over time. Each work contemplates remnants—both sacred and mundane—and the traces they leave in their wake.

Core themes:

  • Remnants - What's left after decay
  • Traces - Marks and signs of passage
  • Memory - Persistence and forgetting
  • Mortality - Endings and what remains
  • The Sacred - Divine remnants, dead gods
  • Infohazards & Cognitohazards - Dangerous knowledge and perception
  • Memetic Entities - Self-replicating ideas with negative reproduction cost, transmitted through living beings

This series shares a universe with Ideocart.
